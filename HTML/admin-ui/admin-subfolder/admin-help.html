Help

Manage and update staff information records to ensure accurate and organized clinic data.

Dashboard

Quick overview of today's appointments

Steps

  1. Click Dashboard in the sidebar.
  2. Review summary widgets (today’s appointments, revenue, notifications).
  3. Click any widget to open the related detail page.
  4. Use date filters to change the reporting period.
  5. Tip: Widgets are interactive — clicking them opens the related module.

Messages

Here's what you need to know to manage your messages

  1. Click Appointments.
  2. View the calendar or list; change view with the date picker (day/week/month).
  3. To add: click New Appointment or a calendar timeslot → fill patient, dentist, service, date/time → Save.
  4. To edit/cancel: click the appointment → choose Edit or Cancel → confirm.
  5. Tip: Use the search box to find appointments by patient name or phone number.

Patient Management

Here's what you need to know to manage your patient

  1. Search for a patient or click Add Patient to create a new record.
  2. Inside a record: update personal info, add appointments, upload documents, view history.
  3. Save changes with Save or Update.
  4. Tip: Use A–Z or filters to browse lists quickly.

Dentist Management

Here's what you need to know to manage your dentist management

  1. Click Dentist Management.
  2. Click Add Dentist → enter name, specialty, contact, working hours → Save.
  3. Edit a dentist to update availability or assigned services.
  4. Assign dentists to appointments from the Appointments module.
  5. Tip: Keep working hours updated to avoid double-booking.

Staff Management

Here's what you need to know to manage your Staff Management

  1. Click Staff Management.
  2. To add staff: Add Staff → name, role, email → Save.
  3. Change roles/permissions from the staff profile.
  4. Reset passwords or deactivate accounts as needed.
  5. Tip: Use roles to control module access.

Inventory Management

Here's what you need to know to manage your Inventory Management

  1. Click Inventory Management.
  2. To add an item: New Inventory Item → name, SKU, quantity, reorder level → Save.
  3. Adjust stock from an item’s page (Adjust Stock → enter change → Save).
  4. Review low-stock list and reorder when necessary.
  5. Tip: Set reorder thresholds to highlight low stock automatically.

Reports

Here's what you need to know to manage your Reports

  1. Click Reports.
  2. Select a report type (appointments, revenue, staff performance).
  3. Set date filters and grouping → Generate.
  4. Export as CSV or PDF using the Export button.
  5. Tip: Save frequently used filters for quick access.

Services

Here's what you need to know to manage your Services

  1. Click Services.
  2. Add a service: Add Service → name, duration, price, description → Save.
  3. Edit services to update price or duration.
  4. Assign services to dentists if needed.
  5. Tip: Accurate durations help avoid scheduling conflicts.
